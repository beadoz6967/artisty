## Musical Style and Sound Analysis

### Origin: Scam Rap (2022)
smokedope2016 started in scam rap — a Detroit-adjacent subgenre characterized by lyrics about digital fraud, cybercrime, and finessing. Genre anchors include Teejayx6, Punchmade Dev, BabyTron. Style markers: offhanded/apathetic flow, bouncy synth-heavy beats, monotonous cadence. smokedope2016's early work (SMOKESHOP, parts of BANDO) draws from this register before transitioning.

### Cloud Rap Shift (2023–present)
By the 2016 album (Dec 2023), the scam rap DNA fades. He repositions into cloud rap: dreamy, woozy, haze-forward. Critic and community consensus credits him with a genuine cloud rap revival for the mid-2020s underground.

### Key Influences and Comparisons
- **Black Kray** — voice tone: "relaxed and comatose"; catchy flows (RYM users, Tier 4)
- **Yung Lean / Bladee (Drain Gang)** — cloud rap lineage, icy and atmospheric (KXUA, Tier 3)
- **Eric Dingus** — ambient underground sound (Last.fm, Tier 4)
- **Chris Travis** — beat-type comparisons from producers (Airbit type beats market)
- **Lil Peep** — Fairfieldd alias occupies same guitar/emo space; but smokedope2016 has "frat-boy sleaziness" where Peep had "wounded sensitivity" (Eli Enis, Tier 3)

### Production Signature
- Spacy, echoing samples
- Witch-house adjacent synth leads
- Heavy hitting drums; icy high-end mixing
- Woozy, muddy, "week-old bongwater" low-end (Eli Enis)
- "Dated Instagram filter" mood — evokes 2012-2016 SoundCloud visual memory
- THE COMEDOWN: darker, slower; house influences stripped; more introspective production

### Lyrical Themes
- Dirtbag leisure: Four Lokos, PSP, COD, hoopin', begging girl to stay in and get high
- Substance use — unashamed, almost lifestyle journalism
- 2010s nostalgia — specific brand-era references (Eastbay shoes, Foamposites, Ozweego, Xbox/gaming culture)
- Faceless identity as recurring concept — addressed directly in "Elite Socks" (protecting people in his life; some are "studyin," some "chief the pen")
- THE COMEDOWN themes: self-destruction consequences, overdose risk, "every night comes to an end," fame without separation from the street, introspection without resolution

### Genre Tags (Cross-Referenced)
Cloud Rap · Wave · Witch House (adjacent) · Emo Rap (Fairfieldd alias) · Scam Rap (early catalog) · Trap (early catalog)
